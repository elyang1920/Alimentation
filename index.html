About Us

At the Alimentation Project in Phillips, many of our rescued pups are waiting to find their fur-ever homes. Many of our adoptable have been rescued from death row, from local pounds, or from abusive environments. They are all vaccinated, dewormed, and neutered and can be visited anytime, just with prior registration. By choosing to adopt, you are giving an animal the chance to find a new home and to feel the love and security they deserve. You are also helping free up more space in our shelters, allowing us to rescue and rehabilitate more animals.
