Eligibility for Adoption

To adopt a dog through Alimentation, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Be legally capable of entering into binding contracts.
  • Provide accurate and truthful information during the application process.
  • Agree to a home visit or background check, if required by the shelter or rescue.

Responsibilities of the Adopter

By adopting a dog, you agree to:

  • Provide a safe, loving, and healthy environment for the dog.
  • Ensure the dog receives proper food, water, shelter, and medical care, including vaccinations and regular vet check-ups.
  • Comply with local pet ownership laws, such as licensing, leash laws, and breed restrictions.
  • Not sell, abandon, or surrender the adopted dog to another person or shelter without first contacting the original shelter or rescue for guidance.
